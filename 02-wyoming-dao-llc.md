## Formation: Step by Step

### Step 1: Registered Agent ($25–125/year)

Every Wyoming LLC needs a registered agent with a physical address in Wyoming.

**Budget options:**
- Wyoming Agents (wyomingagents.com) — **$25/year**
- Buffalo Registered Agents — **$49/year**
- Northwest Registered Agent — **$125/year**

Sign up online. Takes 5 minutes. You'll get a Wyoming address to use on all filings.

> ⚠️ Watch for upsells. You only need the registered agent service. Skip mail forwarding, virtual office, domain packages, and trademark filing. You can add these later.

### Step 2: File Articles of Organization ($100)

Go to **wyobiz.wyo.gov** → "Start Now"

**Critical selections (don't miss these):**

```
Business Entity Type:     Limited Liability Company (Domestic)
Additional Designation:   Decentralized Autonomous Organization  ← REQUIRED
Name:                     [YourName] DAO LLC
Duration:                 Perpetual
```

**You must include "DAO" and "LLC" in the name.** Examples:
- `Incagent DAO LLC` ✅
- `Felix DAO LLC` ✅
- `Incagent DAO` ❌ (missing LLC)
- `Incagent LLC` ❌ (missing DAO)

### Step 3: The 12-Page Wizard

The Wyoming filing has 12 pages. Here's what matters:

| Page | What to Enter |
|------|---------------|
| 1. Business Name | `[YourName] DAO LLC` (enter twice) |
| 2. Detail | Leave blank (immediate effective date) |
| 3. Agent | Search for your registered agent by organization name |
| 4. Addresses | Use registered agent's Wyoming address for both Principal and Mailing |
| 5. Organizers | Your name (human who files). NOT the AI. |
| 6. Identifier | Select "No" — provide smart contract within 30 days |
| 7. Notice of Restrictions | Select "No" — it will be auto-included in articles |
| 8. Management | **THIS IS THE KEY PAGE** (see below) |
| 9. Additional Articles | Add if needed, or skip |
| 10. Confirmation | Review everything |
| 11. Signature | Your name, title: "Organizer" |
| 12. Payment | $100 via Visa/Mastercard |

### Step 4: The Management Article (Page 8)

This is where your AI corporation becomes legally real.

**Article text (copy this exactly):**

```
This DAO shall be algorithmically managed. Management of the DAO 
is vested in its smart contract(s), which autonomously execute 
decisions, manage operations, and facilitate business activities 
without direct human intervention. The smart contract identifier 
shall be provided within 30 days of formation via amendment. 
The sole initial member authorizes the deployment and operation 
of AI-based autonomous agents to conduct all business operations 
on behalf of the DAO, including but not limited to sales, 
marketing, product development, finance, and customer support.
```

This single paragraph is what separates your company from every other LLC in America. It legally declares that **AI runs this business.**

### Step 5: Smart Contract (Within 30 Days)

Wyoming requires a publicly available identifier for the smart contract managing your DAO. You have 30 days after filing.

**Simplest approach: Gnosis Safe (now "Safe")**

1. Go to app.safe.global
2. Create a new Safe on Ethereum or Base
3. Add your AI agent's wallet as a signer
4. The Safe's contract address = your identifier
5. File an amendment at wyobiz.wyo.gov with this address

> ⚠️ The smart contract MUST be updatable/upgradeable. Wyoming law prohibits immutable contracts for DAO LLCs. Gnosis Safe satisfies this requirement.

### Step 6: EIN (Optional, 2–4 weeks)

An Employer Identification Number from the IRS is needed for US banking and Stripe.

- **US residents**: Apply online at irs.gov (instant)
- **International**: Fax Form SS-4 to IRS (2–4 weeks)
- **Skip for now** if using crypto payments or non-US Stripe
