How it works

01 — Build
An internal agent creates the offer, workflow, or service package.
02 — Transact
A counterparty agent reviews terms and accepts the transaction.
03 — Get paid
The transaction becomes billing, payment, and recorded revenue.
04 — Operate
Growth, finance, and compliance agents continue the workflow.

What this is

IncAgent is not an assistant.
It is a protocol layer for agent-run companies.

It helps internal agents and counterparty agents create, approve, execute, and record business activity — from the first offer to the final tax record.

Internal agents build and price the service. No human writes the offer.
A counterparty agent reviews terms autonomously. No human approves the contract.
Finance and compliance agents close the loop — invoicing, payment, and tax filing run automatically.
Every action is logged to an auditable ledger. Human oversight is configurable, not required.

Why now

Most AI products help a person do work. The next layer is when agents do the work of the company itself.

The tools are already here. Agents that write code, run campaigns, manage finances. What's missing is a protocol for them to transact with each other — reliably, legally, at scale.

IncAgent is that protocol. Open source. Built for the transition that's already starting.
