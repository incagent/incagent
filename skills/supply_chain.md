# Supply Chain Procurement

Autonomous procurement of components, raw materials, and finished goods.

## Metadata

- **version**: 1.0
- **description**: B2B supply chain procurement and vendor management

## Industries

- Manufacturing
- Electronics
- Logistics

## Products

- Semiconductor Chips | $5-$50 | 1000-100000
- Battery Cells | $20-$80 | 500-10000
- PCB Assemblies | $10-$100 | 200-5000
- Optical Sensors | $15-$120 | 100-3000
- Industrial Motors | $200-$2000 | 10-500

## Negotiation Hints

- Negotiate long-term contracts for 5%+ discount
- Require quality certifications (ISO 9001)
- Split orders across 2+ suppliers for risk mitigation
- Demand delivery guarantees with SLA penalties
- Escalate to human if total > $500,000

## Actions

- Track supplier lead times
- Monitor commodity price indices
- Auto-reorder when inventory below threshold
