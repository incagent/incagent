## The Three Forces

### 1. Legal Infrastructure Exists

Wyoming recognized DAOs as legal entities in 2021. The law explicitly allows:

- **Algorithmically-managed LLCs** — smart contracts as the management layer
- **Zero-member LLCs** — no human owner required
- **Pass-through taxation** — profits taxed once, not twice

This is not a loophole. It is intentional legislation. Wyoming designed this.

### 2. Autonomous Agents Work

OpenClaw (247K+ GitHub stars) turned "AI chatbot" into "AI employee":

- Runs 24/7 on any machine
- Executes shell commands, manages files, controls browsers
- Sends emails, posts on social media, manages calendars
- Makes decisions without waiting for human approval
- Learns and improves nightly through self-review loops

The gap between "tool" and "worker" closed in 2025. The gap between "worker" and "executive" is closing now.

### 3. Payment Rails Are Ready

- **Visa, Mastercard, PayPal** launched AI agent payment rails (2025)
- **Pay3** enables agent-to-agent stablecoin transactions
- **Google A2A Protocol** + **Anthropic MCP** standardize agent communication
- **Stripe** processes payments for AI-built storefronts

An AI can now earn, spend, and reinvest — programmatically.
